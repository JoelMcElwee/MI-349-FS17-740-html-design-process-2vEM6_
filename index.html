Widdle Wockets

SHORT PITCH

Widdle wocket is a children's developmental learning site that structures it's lessons and activities around STEM education. Our goal is to mold the minds of future astronauts!

LONG PITCH

Our world is constantly growing. We are getting too big for the earth, so we are expanding into space! Right now, the majority of astronauts are naturally talented individuals who have always aspired to be astronauts. Our goal is to present space as a dream for our little rockets! We want to give children the opportunity to see the world as an astronaut does! Another goal of ours is to increase performance in STEM related subjects among children.
